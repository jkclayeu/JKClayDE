The hands behind the clay

Hello, I'm Julia.

I trained as a biologist — a PhD, in fact — but I've always needed to make things with my hands as well as my mind. I started throwing pottery in 2019, and moved from Saint Petersburg to a small town near Nuremberg in 2023, studio and all.

A scientist can't resist an experiment, so my shelves are full of glaze tests and quiet colour studies. Every mug, bowl and vase here is thrown, trimmed, glazed and fired by me. Let's make each of your days a little more special.

Julia K. @jk_and_clay
