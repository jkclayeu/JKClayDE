A handmade gift carries something a boxed set cannot: the sense that someone chose this exact object, made by these exact hands, for this exact person.

When choosing, think about the person's daily rituals rather than their shelf. A big-handled mug for the friend who reads with their coffee. A small vase for the one who brings a single flower home from a walk. The gift lands hardest when it fits a habit they already love.

And do not worry about matching. One beautiful, singular piece is warmer than a set of six identical ones. Its little irregularities are the proof that it is the only one of its kind — much like the person receiving it.
